How to play

Each email is written by someone undermining themselves — hedging, apologising, fishing for reassurance.

The self-defeating phrases are highlighted in red. Scratch over them with your finger or mouse to cross them out.

A stronger version appears in green where needed. Sometimes nothing replaces it — the sentence is simply stronger without.

Clear all the phrases in a letter to move on.
